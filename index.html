What it does

❤️

Health Bar = Battery

9 pixel-art hearts mirror your battery percentage. When it drops — you hear the classic Minecraft hurt sound.

🍗

Hunger Bar = Real Hunger

Empties over 6 hours like actual hunger. 3 quick clicks to eat and refill it — complete with the eating sound.

✨

XP Bar = Your Work

Every keystroke and mouse click earns XP. Level up sounds play automatically. Resets at midnight.

💀

Kill Feed

When you close any app, it shows up in the top-right corner. "Spotify eliminated." "Zoom has fallen." Classic.

🎵

Real Minecraft Sounds

Dirt when you type. Gravel on Delete. TNT on Force Quit. XP pickup on Cmd+S. Villager on Cmd+T. All original game sounds.

🖥️

Always On Top, Never In The Way

Fully transparent overlay. Floats above all apps. Completely click-through — you'll forget it's there. Until it's not.

How to install

01

Download & open the .app

No installer needed. Drag MinecraftHUD.app to your Applications folder or just double-click it.

▶
02

Grant Accessibility Access

System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility → add MinecraftHUD. This is needed for keyboard sounds.

▶
03

Play your life

The HUD appears at the bottom of your screen. Start typing. Close some apps. Hear the sounds. You're in survival mode now.
