Privacy Policy

MinecraftHUD does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

// Overview

MinecraftHUD is a macOS menu bar app that plays Minecraft sounds while you type. Your privacy is straightforward: we collect nothing.

// Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Keystrokes — the app listens for key events to trigger sounds, but no key data is recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.
  • Personal information — no name, email, or account is required to use the app.
  • Usage analytics — no crash reports, telemetry, or analytics are collected.
  • Location data — the app does not access your location.

// Permissions Explained

Accessibility — required to detect keyboard shortcuts (e.g. Cmd+S, Cmd+C) so the matching Minecraft sound can be played. No keystrokes are logged.

Input Monitoring — required to play the dirt sound while you type. No key data is stored or transmitted.

// Third Parties

MinecraftHUD does not share data with any third parties because no data is collected in the first place. The app makes no network requests during normal use.

// Purchase

Purchases are processed by Stripe or the Mac App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by those platforms — MinecraftHUD never sees or stores payment data.
